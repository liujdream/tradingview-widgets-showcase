Pre-Market & Post-Market Data Display

🎯 Symbol Info Widget - Best for Extended Hours

The Symbol Info widget (compact "quote card") is the optimal choice for displaying pre-market and post-market data.

✅ Shows regular session price ("At close")

✅ Displays Pre Market/Post Market prices when extended session is active

✅ Includes timestamp for extended hours data

✅ Compact design perfect for dashboards

Implementation Guide

When to Use Symbol Info Widget

• Building a pre-market dashboard

• Showing after-hours trading activity

• Displaying comprehensive quote information

• Creating compact quote cards for multiple symbols

Extended Hours Trading Times (US Markets)

• Pre-Market: 4:00 AM - 9:30 AM ET

• Regular Session: 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET

• Post-Market: 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM ET

Note: Extended hours data availability depends on the exchange and symbol.
